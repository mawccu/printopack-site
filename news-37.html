General Information · 05/04/2022

The mixture of high levels of raw material and energy costs and availability challenges the European Flexible Packaging industry.

← Back to news

Energy and raw material prices were already at a very high level but increased even more after the beginning of the war in Ukraine.

This rise has a serious financial impact on the flexible packaging supply chain, all of the main materials used in Flexible Packagings such as plastics, paper, and aluminum are worrying, as well as the adhesive, lacquers and inks are at risk.

The industry itself is faced with high energy prices in their direct operations manufacturing flexible packaging.

Due to the significant increase in diesel prices, reports show the risk of reduction of available logistic capacities as companies will have to give up their operational business.
